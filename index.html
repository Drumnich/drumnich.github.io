Our Story

The journey of Mr. Delicious

In 2022, I embarked on an exciting adventure that would change my life and the culinary landscape of Helsinki. With a deep love for street food and a desire to share my passion with the community, I launched my food truck, bringing the vibrant flavors of street cuisine to the heart of the city.

From the bustling streets of Helsinki Centre to various festivals and events, I have had the privilege of serving countless customers. Each dish is crafted with care, inspired by the rich tapestry of flavors that street food has to offer. The warm response from the community has been overwhelming; it's the love and enthusiasm of my customers that have fueled my growth.

As my food truck continues to roll through the streets of Helsinki, I remain dedicated to providing delicious, high-quality meals that bring people together. Thank you for being a part of this incredible journey. I look forward to serving you the best street food that our city has to offer!
